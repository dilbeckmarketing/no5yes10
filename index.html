ABOUT "clothes the deal" clothing drive at dilbeck real estate

November 14th - December 14th | "Clothes the Deal" Clothing Drive

Dilbeck Real Estate will be collecting gently used business clothing at all 14 offices for it’s annual clothing drive, November 14 - December 14.  Donations will benefit “Clothes the Deal”, a local charity helping low income and disabled people during their search for a new job or better employment opportunities by providing business clothing and accessories.

Please join us in providing job seekers with the professional clothing they need to rejoin the workforce and reinvent their lives.
